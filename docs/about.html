About Me

Who I Am

My name is Dorian Bailey and I'm a Junior at Penn State studying Marketing and Management Information Systems (MIS). I'm interested in the ways technology, data, and creativity come together to shape how businesses communicate and connect with people.

I built this website as part of DIGIT 100: Introduction to Digital Humanities at Penn State. Through this course I've learned the basics of HTML, CSS, version control with Git and GitHub, and what it means to work with digital tools in an academic and professional context.

My Interests

Outside of academics, I'm drawn to digital media, design, and the evolving landscape of AI technology. I enjoy working with Adobe Photoshop to create and edit visuals, and I'm increasingly interested in AI engineering — exploring how generative AI tools can be applied creatively and strategically.

I also have hands-on professional experience in social media marketing through my internship at Empannisahs, where I worked on content strategy and digital outreach. Balancing that with my work as a server at Skunk and Goat Tavern has given me a strong foundation in both communication and working under pressure.

My Goals

I want to build a career at the intersection of marketing and technology — using data, design, and digital tools to help organizations tell better stories and reach the right audiences. I'm particularly interested in how AI is transforming digital marketing and content creation.

You can view my full background on my résumé page, or explore my gallery to see some of my work and interests.
